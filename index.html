Welcome to Mahjongg Solitaire!

Mahjongg Solitaire is a classic tile-matching puzzle game that tests your pattern recognition and strategic planning. Match identical tiles to clear the board and challenge yourself with various layouts and difficulty levels.

In Mahjongg Solitaire, your goal is to remove all tiles from the board by matching pairs of identical tiles. But be careful - you can only select tiles that are "free" (not blocked by other tiles on either side) and on top of a stack. Plan your moves carefully to avoid getting stuck!

With beautiful tile designs, multiple layouts, and addictive gameplay, Mahjongg Solitaire is easy to learn but challenging to master. Test your skills with traditional layouts or try our special challenges for an extra brain workout.
